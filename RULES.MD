General Rules

College ID is mandatory.
Participants must avoid the use of offensive language, indecent behaviour, and inappropriate attire during the competitions; failure to comply will result in disqualification. 
The use of cigarettes, alcohol, or any prohibited substances is strictly forbidden.
Judge’s and Organizer’s decisions are the final.
Participants should go through the rules and adhere to the specifics. 
Participation, props, music, etc., for certain events should be confirmed during registration. If required, contact the event heads.
All participants are allowed to participate in more than one event as long as the event timings do not clash.
The event will commence at the scheduled time.
Half an hour after the commencement of the event, no participant shall be allowed to enter the event hall.
USBs should be submitted to the event coordinators prior to the event (If any).
The faculties, event coordinators, and judges have the right to disqualify a participant in case of violation of any rules.

NOTE:
For a team event, a minimum of five teams must be present inside the event venue for the event to begin.
For an individual event, a minimum of ten participants must be present inside the event venue for the event to begin.
Prize amounts are subject to change based on the number of participants. 
Event rounds will be subject to change based on the total participant count and the judges decision.
